Privacy Policy for crip

Last updated: July 5, 2026

crip ("the app") is a private, encrypted file and contact vault for iOS. This policy explains what data the app handles and, just as importantly, what it does not.

Summary

  • crip does not have a server. It never sends your files, contacts, or content anywhere.
  • crip does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • crip does not collect or share personal data with the developer or any third party.

Data you create in the app

Files and contacts you add to crip are encrypted and stored only in local app storage on your device (and, for the share extension, in a storage container shared between crip and the extension on the same device). This data never leaves your device, is never uploaded to a server, and is never accessible to the developer.

Face ID / device authentication

crip may use Face ID or your device passcode as an additional check before decrypting files protected with your public key. This authentication happens entirely on-device using Apple's frameworks — crip never receives or stores biometric data.

Photo library access

If you choose to save a decrypted image out of crip, the app requests permission to add that image to your Photos library. crip only writes the image you explicitly choose to save; it does not read or browse your existing photo library.

In-app purchases

crip offers optional subscriptions and a lifetime unlock, processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit. Payment information is handled by Apple according to Apple's own privacy policy — crip never sees or stores your payment details. crip only knows whether a purchase is active, so it can unlock the corresponding features.

Third parties

crip does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. Aside from Apple's StoreKit for purchases, the app does not communicate with any external service.

Children's privacy

crip does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
